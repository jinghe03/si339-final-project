Who Am I?

As a Senior at the University of Michigan, I look fondly back at the times where I had the opportunity to explore Ann Arbor's diverse culinary scenes on and off campus. From dining out with loved ones -- whether that be friends' birthday celebrations, girls' night out, parents weekend, and more -- I have cultivated a deep appreciation for food, culture, and dining experiences, which I share through these recommendations and personal reviews!
